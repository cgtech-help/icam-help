.. _general_information:

General Information
*******************

This chapter provide a general overview of Icam Post and describes its two main applications:
The first is QUEST, a questionnaire based post-processor developer; the second is GENER, the
run-time post-processor that converts CAD/CAM system output data to CNC machine input
programs.
