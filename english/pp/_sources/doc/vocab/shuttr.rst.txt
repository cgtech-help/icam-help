.. _command_shuttr:

The SHUTTR Command :kbd:`[PC]`
==============================

The SHUTTR command is used to control the laser beam shutter. It is
available with punch press machines with contouring attachments and
contouring machines that are of type “Laser”.

   :math:`\textbf{SHUTTR /} \begin{pmatrix}\begin{array}{l} \textbf{ON} \\ \textbf{OFF} \end{array}\end{pmatrix}`

Specification of either ON or OFF will output the appropriate shutter
open or close code.
