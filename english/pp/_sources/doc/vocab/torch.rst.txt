.. _command_torch:

The TORCH Command :kbd:`[PC]`
=============================

The TORCH command is used to control the torch status. It is
available with punch press machines with contouring attachments and
contouring machines that are of type “Flame”.

   :math:`\textbf{TORCH /} \begin{pmatrix}\begin{array}{l} \textbf{ON} \\ \textbf{OFF} \end{array}\end{pmatrix}`

Specify TORCH/ON to start the cutting process. Specify TORCH/OFF to
stop the cutting process.
