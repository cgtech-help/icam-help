.. _command_power:

The POWER Command :kbd:`[PC]`
=============================

The POWER command is used to control power settings on hybrid punch
press machines with contouring attachments and contouring machines.

   :math:`\textbf{POWER / } \mathit{flow}`

   :math:`\textbf{POWER / } \Big[\,\big[\,\textbf{WATTS},\big]\,w \,\Big] \,
   \begin{bmatrix} , \begin{pmatrix}\begin{array}{l} \textbf{PULSE} \\ \textbf{CONST} \end{array}\end{pmatrix} \big[,a \,\big] \end{bmatrix} \;
   \big[,\textbf{PERSEC},\mathit{freq} \,\big]`

   :math:`\textbf{POWER / } \begin{pmatrix}\begin{array}{l} \textbf{ON} \\ \textbf{OFF} \end{array}\end{pmatrix}`

For flame cutting, the *flow* parameter specifies the flame intensity
or power setting.

For laser cutting: WATTS specifies the power setting; PULSE and CONST
indicate either a pulsed width or constant wave beam; and PERSEC
specifies the pulse frequency.

Specify OFF to turn the laser or flame off. Specify ON to turn the
flame or laser back on at the previously specified settings.
