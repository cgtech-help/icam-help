Save/Generate
-------------

|image1|

This dialog appears when exiting QUEST and there is one or more
documents that have been modified and not saved. You have the option of
saving, generating or ignore the changes of one document at a time or
you can choose the *All* version of the options to apply your choice to
all pending unsaved documents.
